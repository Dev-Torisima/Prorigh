What is Prorigh?

What can you do with this?

By simply moving and connecting Blocks, you can virtually program.

What are the specifications?

You can use things from variables, constants and functions, to outputs, drawing and file operations. You can see here for more details.

Are there any samples?

There are ones that give you an overview of a block, and ones that calculate numerical sequences. You can get them here.

Privacy is very important!
